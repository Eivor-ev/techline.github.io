My Rules

These are the rules I live by / expect in my projects / community / server, etc.

  1. Be respectful – No toxicity, harassment, or personal attacks.
  2. Stay on topic – Keep discussions relevant.
  3. No spam or self-promotion – Ads, referral links, or excessive self-promo will be removed.
  4. Use English (or whatever language you want) – So everyone can understand.
  5. No illegal content – Piracy, leaks, etc. are forbidden.
  6. Help others when you can – We grow together.

Consequences

  • First offense → Warning
  • Second offense → Temporary mute/ban
  • Serious or repeated offenses → Permanent ban
